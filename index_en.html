Amateur Radio Diploma Action Promoting the Activities of the Stargard Science Center "FILARY" in Stargard.

RULES
  1. ORGANIZERS

    Municipal Amateur Radio Club PZK "MIEDWIE" SP1KM and Stargard Science Center "FILARY".

  2. OBJECTIVES
    • Promotion of the Stargard Science Center "FILARY" in Stargard - https://scn-filary.pl/, https://www.facebook.com/scn.filary, https://www.instagram.com/scn.filary/
    • Promotion of amateur radio and the Municipal Amateur Radio Club PZK "MIEDWIE" SP1KM at the Science Picnic organized by SCN "FILARY".
  3. DATE

    From the Wednesday preceding the Science Picnic organized by the Stargard Science Center "FILARY" to the second Wednesday, inclusive, after the Science Picnic. The date of the Science Picnic: 28-09-2024

  4. CONDITIONS FOR RECEIVING THE E-DIPLOMA
    • For SP stations, collect 25 points – mandatory QSO with HF1SCN station
    • For foreign stations, 20 points – mandatory QSO with HF1SCN station
    • For satellite communications, only 1 QSO with HF1SCN station is required
    • For SWL stations, the same conditions apply as for SP stations.
  5. SCORING

    During the diploma action, the special event station HF1SCN will be operational.

    • For QSO with HF1SCN station - 15 points
    • For QSO with SP1KM station - 10 points
    • For QSO with members of the SP1KM club, i.e., SP1MIR, SP1JMF, SQ1M, SP1IMJ, SP1JNX, SP1KK – 5 points each.
  6. MODES

    Stations will operate on HF, VHF, UHF, and SAT bands using SSB, CW, FM, and DIGI modes.

    QSOs with the same callsign and the same mode can be repeated each day on different bands. QSOs with the same callsign can also be repeated on subsequent days on the same band using the same and different modes.

  7. SUBMISSIONS
    • After collecting the required points, send an email with your submission, including the station callsign in the subject line.
    • Sending a log is only required for SWL stations.
    • The submission should be sent within 30 days after the end of the diploma action to the email: contact@sp1km.club
    • Submissions sent after the deadline will not be considered!

Diplomas will be sent progressively as submissions are verified via return email, but no later than 60 days after the diploma action ends.

In the case of a large number of submissions, verification and diploma sending may be delayed.

We wish you fun Vy 73.
